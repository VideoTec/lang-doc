RxJava is a library for 
1）the Observable abstraction and 
2）related higher-order functions. 
It allows us to meet 4/5 of the criteria above with little effort and 
it supports the functional-reactive programming paradigm in Java.

At this point,
it’s fairly well battle-tested on Android. 
It’s been in production in the SoundCloud app for over two years and 
it’s used by several other companies 
like Trello, Square and The New York Times.
