示例

println 'Hello, World!'

解释

Semicolons are optional. If you add them, they work, but they’re not required

Parentheses are optional until they’re not. 
If the compiler guesses correctly where they should have gone, everything works. 
Otherwise, add them back in. 
The println method takes a String argument. 
Here the parentheses are left out.

There are two types of strings in Groovy: 
1) single-quoted strings, like Hello, are instances of java.lang.String. 
2) Double-quoted strings are Groovy strings and allow interpolation, shown in Example A-2.
