parentheses 圆括号

Groovy's syntax permits omitting parentheses and dots in some situations. 
The following groovy code

···
take(coffee).with(sugar, milk).and(liquor)
···

can be written as

···
take coffee with sugar, milk and liquor
···

enabling the development of domain-specific languages (DSLs) 
which look like plain English.
